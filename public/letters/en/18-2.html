Whenever I think about this every now and again, I feel this terrible sense of fear. This world is overflowing with things I do not know, and in the grand scale of things, they definitely outscale my meagre lifetime. Sensei, is there no way for my hot-air balloon to make it to space? This reminds me of a word that I found in a book I was reading, the word Aporia. It refers to something that has no apparent resolution. Perhaps Sensei would consider my desire for knowledge to be an Aporia. As usual, I have attached my poem on a different page. Please take care of yourself. Sincerely, P.S. I noticed that you send out your letters on specific days, don’t you? I find it strange that your letters always get to my mailbox on a Sunday. It’s a Sunday after all. Sensei, did you notice? This too, is somewhat of an Aporia, isn’t it?
