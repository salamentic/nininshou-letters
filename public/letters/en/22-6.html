October 6

Dear Sensei, Am I really moving forward, bit by bit? Can I really say that’s true? I’ve always thought that I lived like a corpse, just going through the motions. Even now, I can’t say that’s changed. Writing poems, creating melodies—I only do them because I want to do something noble. I just want to close the gap between myself and others. Every day, I just wake up, stroll through the midday streets, write poems on my genkouyoshi, and go to sleep. It almost feels like I’m faking life. Yet I also had a sense that I was wrong with something, somehow. It began when I found your flyer. My mother handed it to me in the living room and said “well, if you’re going to be writing, you might as well try sending something in.” I have to do some thinking. I have to go find something around the house. You don’t have to reply. I will write again. Please take care.

Sincerely,
