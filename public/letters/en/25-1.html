Dear Sensei  Sensei, it has been some days already. How have you been? This is the first time I have sent you four letters in a row. Lately, I’ve frequently been thinking about crime and the punishment it receives. And of course, I’ve been writing poems as well. I wrote two, but I wrote a lot about Sensei in one of them. Just after finishing it, I thought this wasn’t right, so I abandoned that poem.  So, I left just this one poem in the envelope. The title is “Moonbath”. I often imagine sceneries like this, those that make me think that things can come like this eventually.  I will send you another poem.    Sincerely yours.
