Dear boy, The season of late autumn frost has come, and fallen leaves now lie scattered everywhere. The mornings and evenings have grown sharply colder, and with each passing day I can feel winter drawing nearer. I hope you are well and continuing in good health. The other day, I met with your mother. There, I heard everything. So, in the end, you did speak with your mother. Thank you for the many, many letters you sent, even during the time when I could not send any reply. Thank you for thinking so deeply about my situation, for worrying over it and trying to reach your own answer. Truly… Thank you. It simply made me happy. Let us end our correspondence here. You are about to set out to find your own true words. When you do, my words will surely no longer be necessary. Before I knew it, you had grown very strong. That makes me happy. Truly. So much so that it brings tears to my eyes. During the years since my imprisonment, I have spent a great deal of time thinking about crime and punishment. Because of my own weakness, I hurt your grandmother and betrayed my family.
