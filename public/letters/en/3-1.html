This time, I'm showing you this. I enclosed it in the envelope. The photo included is of a sunset I came across recently. The camera is something I found in my mother's library and borrowed secretly. The melody that came to me was more vivid than what would usually come. I took out the guitar I have at home and played chords while thinking about the notes.   Sensei, from what I could tell from your letter yesterday, you seem like someone familiar with music. When I think that the words I write could be expressed along with a musical scale, it helps me feel less embarrassed about my poem. To say that it possesses music is, in other words, allowing myself to become familiar with my level of communicating poetic compositions. Don't you feel so too? Maybe that’s just me.
