Forget me A heart for me, a bouquet for you, Just your swaying hair, let it keep swaying that way. In our little house inside a box, in the kitchen we arranged together, Beyond the small window’s curtains, in our garden of memories, Bury one long autumn night Plant a single ray of spring sunshine, water it a bit from time to time, When the loquat fruit is ready, forget me please. To me, to me, to me A heart for me, and a bouquet of flowers for you, Just your swaying hair, let it keep swaying that way. Words for me, and your quiet humming, Beyond a long, long labyrinth, just leave it there. Try counting them one by one, the things in your life, Things that you want to love.
