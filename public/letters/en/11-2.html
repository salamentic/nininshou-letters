What else should I recommend? Since you seem interested in the modern period, how about these: Rainer Maria Rilke A poet born in Prague. The original texts are in German. Charles Baudelaire The Flowers of Evil, Paris Spleen A French poet who directly influenced Hagiwara Sakutarō. If "The Flowers of Evil” is a collection of verse poems, then "Paris Spleen” is more prose-like. "Love Poetry Collection” by Muro Saisei What really stands out is his poem titled "Sin.” It's a short poem, but to me it seems to possess a powerful impact; it often comes to my mind. Even Chinese poetry is good. The most popular poets are Li Bai and Du Fu, but I also like Yuan Zhen.
