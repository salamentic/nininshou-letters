Dear Sensei, This is the first time I've sent 3 letters in a row. It's been a while since then. How have you been? I've heard about Sensei from my mother. So you are an acquaintance of my mother, huh. She had known about you. I didn't know until my mother told me that the house you broke into was my grandfather's. She had never said a word about it before, and I never asked it to my grandfather either. Perhaps she didn't want to make a fuss of it, or didn't want me to worry. You broke into the home of an acquaintance's parents. You said the house you stole from was the house next door, didn't you? Then of course, you must have known who lived there. The person you hurt was my grandmother. I'm not saying this to blame you or to condemn you. I just can't stop wondering, what were you feeling at the time? Sensei, what kind of a person are you? I've been thinking since I knew the truth. And I'm confused. I cannot imagine Sensei who wrote gentle words could be someone who committed a crime.
