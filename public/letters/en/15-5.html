Understood. To be honest, it’s exactly how Sensei put it. For some reason, I figured you would eventually come to realize it as well. I can’t write anything decent unless I have a theme to work with. Be it a theme obtained from a book, or a theme given to me by Sensei, or a theme that comes to me from my day-to-day environment. You might say that is only natural, but in my case it’s worse than that. I only became aware of it recently. I don’t have my own words. The reason why the library feels so comforting is because it’s filled with other people’s words. Referencing is just an excuse. I make a foundation of other people’s words and put my own words as a supplement, just that cycle repetitively. Still, if referencing other people’s words is just a pencil, then I feel a little saved. Thank you. From now on, I’ll try to use only the things that came out from within myself. So, what theme should I write next?
