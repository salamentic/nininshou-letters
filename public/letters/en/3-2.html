When you hear the word “devilishness,” Sensei, what comes to your mind? What comes to mine first is the colour red. I like warm colours. I like orange, vermillion, a simple red and even a loess-esque yellow. Pink isn’t my cup of tea, but I don’t hate it. Out of all them, my favourite might be the dull red of a sunset right before night falls. The kind of colour mixed with night- that one. The echo that the word “Devilishness” holds.   That is surely a wildfire. Someone sets fire to the spring field from so far away that I cannot see it with my own eyes. That light that was lit over there ignites that wild red.

That wildfire is a flame that grows so much that it will slowly get closer and cover the whole city where I live before we realize it. Every time I look at the sunset, I always imagine that. I never get tired of viewing that simple, pure red that burns everything up.. The cherry blossoms are beginning to bloom, and the joyful spring has already begun.
