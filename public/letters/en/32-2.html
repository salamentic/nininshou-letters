The fact that mother went that far to connect me to sensei. From that fact, what I can think of is that both my mother and I are related to you. Sensei, who are you? Someone my grandfather knew, someone my mother felt she wanted to forgive. Sensei said that you lived next to my grandfather, didn’t you? I now know that wasn’t true. When I went to see my grandfather the other day, I looked at the house next door. My memory was vague, but all that was there was an empty lot and a field. My grandfather avoided the subject, but it was clear that you were not a neighbor. My father is already dead. I’ve confirmed that . He had no siblings. Within six degree of kinship would include even the siblings of my grandparents, but my maternal grandparents are all from Kyushu and we have no relatives we keep in contact with. So I began to think within the range of three degrees of kinship, especially on my mother’s side. My mother has a younger brother. That would be my uncle. The guitar and camera that we had in my home were apparently entrusted to us by him. I’ve hardly ever met my relatives so I don’t know his face. But still, “what if?” is what I've been thinking.
