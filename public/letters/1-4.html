Even The Flowers Stir The wind passes through you and me Even the flowers stir during midday rain And you are a softly smiling pure-white cloud. The wind passes through, scorching me Even the waves are colored by the shadow of the evening sun And I too, like you, am the summer wind. Rising and falling amidst headwinds Farewell to all the days spent waiting for summer. Missing the nostalgic midsummer waves As I stay dreaming, dreaming again. Tired of waiting, I head towards the sea Even the moon that should be shining now hides itself away Leaving only your pure white sleeves glowing. Summer passes by, holding me here The wind rustles, as if forgetting today. Taking only you and me away.
